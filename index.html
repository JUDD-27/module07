Our Story

For over 25 years, Luigi’s has been more than just a restaurant — it’s been a family table. Every recipe is inspired by the flavors of southern Italy, carried across generations, and served with pride. From the first loaf of warm bread to the last bite of tiramisu, we welcome you as neighbors and send you off as friends.
